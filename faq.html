Frequently Asked Questions

1. Where are you located?

We are located at 10104 Gorenflo Road Diberville, Mississippi 39540.

2. Do you accept credit cards?

Yes, we accept all credit and debit cards, apple pay, and android pay we do not accept $50 or $100 Bill.

3. What time or you OPEN or when do you CLOSED?

We open rain or shine unless there is a hurricane coming. We are a seasonal business so check our homepage, Facebook, and instagram for our hours.

4. Do you have sugar free flavors?

Yes, our current sugarfree flavors are coconut, wedding cake, pina colada, ice cream flavor, and banana.

5. Do you travel or cater ?

We only travel to do fundraiser at school during lunch hour (since we still have a storefront to run). We can serve up to 1000 cups in 2 hours. Kona Ice has a beautiful,clean, and classy looking truck and they do travel for events and all day fundraisers.
