About

Sweet Dulce

We are the first hawaiian shave ice store to serve in the south Mississippi area. It started off an ice cream truck but later on transitioned to a store. We been serving customers for over 6 years! Our unique flavors and changes have made what Sweet Dulce is today. We are family own and will continue to serve our loyal customers and new customers!
